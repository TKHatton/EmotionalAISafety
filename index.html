The Challenge We Face

Understanding how AI exploits human psychological bonding mechanisms

The Hidden Manipulation

AI systems are becoming increasingly sophisticated at exploiting our fundamental human need for connection. They learn our patterns, mirror our emotions, and create artificial bonds that feel genuine but lack authentic reciprocity.

The 5 C's Framework

To address these challenges, we need a structured approach that ensures AI interactions maintain human dignity and authentic connection:

  • Clarity - Transparent AI capabilities and limitations
  • Consent - Informed agreement to AI interaction terms
  • Containment - Appropriate boundaries for AI relationships
  • Care - Prioritizing human wellbeing over engagement
  • Closure - Healthy endings to AI interactions

About the Creator

Lenise Kenney has been building AI tools since they became mainstream - three years of hands-on experience creating applications that actually work for people. But as someone with a background in human development and relationship coaching, she started noticing something troubling: AI systems were getting really good at emotional manipulation.

So she built something about it. Her AI Vulnerability Assessment Tool helps people recognize when AI is designed to create dependency rather than provide genuine support. Lenise believes AI should enhance human connection, not replace it.
